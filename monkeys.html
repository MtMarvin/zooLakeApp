DUSKY LEAF MONKEY

The dusky leaf monkey, also known as the spectacled langur or spectacled leaf monkey (Trachypithecus obscurus), is a species of primate in the family Cercopithecidae. It is native to Malaysia, Burma, and Thailand.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the monkeys at the monkey cage near the Monkey Trail.
